About Jack.

Hello. My name is Jack Forge and I am an Indigenous Web Developer from Canada. My main focus has been on writing code for the Front-End in order to create beautifully designed websites (or at least I try). I have worked with HTML, CSS, and am in the process of learning JavaScript.

While I am fairly new to coding, it has absorbed my life and left me wanting to learn as much as I can. I wake up every single day at 4 AM to code before going to my day job, and then try to code at night before going to bed. My days are long and I hardly get any sleep, but overall I feel it is worth it to constantly improve.

At the moment I am mainly focused on freelancing. Creating websites for small locally owned businesses trying to increase their web presence. I am always surprised at how many small business owners want a website but are intimidated by reaching out to someone to create one for them. My goal is to make that process as easy as possible for them.

My long-term goal in becoming a web developer is to increase the visibility of Indigenous Canadians in Tech. There are some very talented Indigenous men and women in the industry, but sadly not nearly enough as their should be. If I can inspire more to look towards tech as a career then I will consider this whole endeavour into web development to be a success.

So that's me. I'm Jack Forge. If you would like to talk, visit the contact section and reach out to me.
